Leaving on a Jet Plane

34 bar strathspey for 3 couples in a triangular set

by Tim Harrison (2005)

Bars     Directions
1-8   All dance interlocking reels of four. Pass partner by the right shoulder to begin.
9-16   First man and first woman dance a figure of eight around their partner's position and their corner.
17-32   All dance a Schiehallion Reel: All men travel to next woman's place on their right while all women dance round their right shoulders into their partners' places (2 bars). All women travel to next woman's place on their right while all men dance round their right shoulder into the man's position (2 bars). Repeat bars 24-27 three times. Finish with all couples progressed one position to the right.
33-34   All turn partner with right hand once round.

Recommended tune: Leaving on a Jet Plane by Me First and the Gimmee Gimmees on their Have a Ball album.

Note: I got an urge to write a Scottish country dance to some unusual music (punk rock).
