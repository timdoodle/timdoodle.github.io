Reely Quick Across

16 bar reel for 3 couples in a longwise set

by Tim Harrison (2001)

Bars     Directions
1-8   First couple cross with right hands, cast off one place, and dance a half figure of eight around the second cuple. Second couple step up on bars 3 & 4.
9-16   First woman dance a left shoulder reel of three with the second couple, finish in second place. First man dance a left shoulder reel of three with the third couple, finish in second place.
Repeat from new position.

Recommended tune: Any good reel.

Note: This is a teaching dance. It provides practice with reel of three across the dance.
