Strathspeys for 5 couples

#1 32 bars (doesn't work real well)

1-4 1s and 3s cast off one place, turn with right hand 3/4 times.
5-8 1W (3W) dance up between 2s (4s) and cast off on own side to face 2nd corner. 1M (3M) dance down between 4s (5s) and cast up to face 2nd corner.
9-12 1s and 3s dance half reel of four with 2nd corners, turn partner with left hand 3/4 times at end of half reel to face 1st corner positions. Dancers in 1M position (2M) and 5W position (5W) dance clockwise around set to change places.
13-16 1s and 3s dance half reel of four with dancers in 1st corner positions, turn partner with left hand 3/4 times at end of half reel to face 1st corner positions. Dancers in 1W position (4M) and 5M position (4W) dance clockwise around set to change places.
17-20 1s and 3s dance half reel of four with dancers in 2nd corner positions, finish (without turning) in the center of the set facing out opposite side. Dancers in 1M position (5M) and 5W position (2W) dance clockwise around set to change places.
21-24 1W gives right shoulder to 2M (in 1M position) to dance half reel of three. 1M gives right shoulder to 4W (in 3W position) and 3W gives right shoulder to 4M (in 3M position) to dance half reel of four, 1W & 3M finish by passing left shoulders without turning. 3M gives right shoulder to 5W (in 5W position) to dance half reel of three.
25-28 1W with 2s, 1M & 3W with 4s, 3M with 5s dance right hands across.
29-32 1s & 3s cast off one place, cross with right hand.

#2 32 bars (based on Linton Ploughman)

1-8 2W with1s, 2M & 4W with 3s, 4M with 5s circle hands round and back.
9-16 2W with 1s, 2M & 4W with 3s, M4 with 5s dance right hands across and back.
17-24 1s & 3s dance down the middle and back. 2s & 4s step in on bar 24.
25-28 1s and 2s, 3s and 4s dance half poussette. 5s step in on bar 28.
29-32 1s and 4s, 3s and 5s dance half poussette.

#3 40 bars

1-4 1s & 3s turn partner with right hand, and cast off one place. 2s and 4s step up on bars 3 & 4.
5-8 1s & 3s turn partner with left hand 1 1/4 times. Finish in the center of the set.
9-16 1W with 2s, 1M & 3W with 4s, and 3M with 5s dance right hands across and back.
17-24 1s and 3s turn partner with right hand, set to partner with women facing down and men facing up, turn partner with both hands, pull back on right shoulder.
25-32 1W with 2s, 1M & 3W with 4s, and 3M with 5s circle hands round and back. All finish on sides of set.
33-40 1s (3s) dance half figure of eight around the 2s (4s), cross with right hand, cast off one place.

#4 32 bars

1-8 1s & 3s turn with right hand, cast off one place while the 2s and 4s step up, and turn with both hands 3/4 times, pull back on right shoulder. Finish back-to-back with partner in the center of the set, women face up, men face down.
9-16 1W with2s, 1M & 3W with 4s, 3M with 5s circle hands round and back. 1s and 3s finish in the center of the set.
17-24 1s and 3s dance reel of four.
25-30 1s & 3s turn partner with right hand 1 1/4 times, cast off one place (on own side).
31-32 2s, 4s, 1s, 5s and 3s set to partner.
