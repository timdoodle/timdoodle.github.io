Springtime in Austin (Draft)

last eight bars don't work

32 bar reel for 4 couples in a longwise set

by Tim Harrison (2000)

Bars     Directions
1-4   First and fourth couples petronella turn and set to each other up and down the dance. Second (Third) couple step up (down) and set to each other.
5-8   First and fourth couples petronella turn and set to each other, pull back on right shoulder to face second (third) couple.
9-16   First and second couples dance a reel of four across the set; while third and fourth couples dance a reel of four across the set. On the last bar of the reel, first (fourth) couple turn with left hand to face down (up) in promenade hold between the second (third) couple. First man has partner on his right. Fourth man has partner on his left.
17-23   First (fourth) couple dances a reel of three on the side, first (fourth) couple giving left shoulder to third man (second woman).
24-27   First and fourth couples dance right hands across.
28-32   First and and fourth couples dance left hands across half-way and set to partners.
    Repeat from new positions.

Recommended tune: Any good reel.

Note: This dance was written in celebration of Spring in Austin, Texas.
